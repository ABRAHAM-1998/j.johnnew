INTRODUCTION


J. John Ltd Care Services is a care agency that provides qualitative and compassionate care with a team of professionals who are fully trained to meet the challenges in the care settings. We provide registered nurses and health care assistants to care home and nursing homes. We promote dignified care with competitive team committed to perform.

DIVERSE SOLUTIONS


J. John Ltd Care Services has experience with diverse customer base. We offer diverse care staff to your service to meet user-based demands. We can support your nursing and caring demands with dementia friendly staff. Our team comprises of diverse ethical and cultural backgrounds. Enhanced DBS (Disclosure of Barring Services) check is carried out for all staff and a staff profile can be provided upon request.

FLEXIBILE SOLUTIONS
